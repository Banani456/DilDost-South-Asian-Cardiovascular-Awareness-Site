Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Water 2 cups
Milk 1 cup
Fresh ginger 1-inch piece (grated or finely chopped)
Black tea leaves or tea bags 2 teaspoons or 2 tea bags
Cardamom pods 2-3 (crushed)
Sweetener (sugar, honey, or stevia) To taste

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, bring the water and milk to a boil.
  2. Add the cardamom pods as the milk begins to boil. Once boiling, add in the grated ginger.
  3. Add the black tea leaves and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes, allowing the tea to infuse.
  4. Once the chai starts to boil, reduce the heat and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Strain the chai into cups to remove the tea leaves and spices.
  6. Add sweetener to taste and stir well.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your Ginger Chai!
