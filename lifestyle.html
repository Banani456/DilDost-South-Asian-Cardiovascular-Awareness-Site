Lifestyle Improvements

Lifestyle adjustments are crucial for a better heart-healthy life, especially for South Asians, who are at a higher risk of cardiovascular diseases due to genetic predispositions and lifestyle factors. Adopting a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can help manage cholesterol levels and blood pressure. Regular physical activity, such as walking or yoga, can improve cardiovascular fitness and reduce stress. Limiting intake of sugary, salty, and fatty foods, along with avoiding tobacco and excessive alcohol, further contributes to heart health.

These adjustments not only mitigate genetic risks but also promote overall well-being, leading to a longer and healthier life. Use this exercise page linked below as a resource to find links to workouts that can help you on your journey to a happier, healthier hearty life!
