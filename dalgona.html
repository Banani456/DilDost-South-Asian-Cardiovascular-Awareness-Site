Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Instant coffee 2 tablespoons
Hot water 2 tablespoons
Stevia 1-2 teaspoons (adjust to taste)
Milk 1 cup (any kind)
Ice (optional) As needed

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the instant coffee, hot water, and stevia.
  2. Using a hand mixer or a whisk, whip the mixture until it becomes thick and frothy. This may take a few minutes.
  3. Fill a glass with milk and add ice if desired.
  4. Spoon the whipped coffee mixture over the milk.
  5. Stir well before drinking and enjoy!
