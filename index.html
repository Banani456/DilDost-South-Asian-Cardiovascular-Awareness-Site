Dil Dost

Your Dil is your Forver Dost

DilDost, meaning "Heart Friend" in English, is a dedicated website aimed at educating South Asian Americans on improving their heart health. Recognizing the high susceptibility of South Asians to cardiovascular diseases, DilDost condenses research from study groups like MASALA to provide up-to-date information and research. The website's goal is to offer effective methods for improving cardiovascular health in the South Asian community, helping individuals improve their lifestyle through culturally relevant approaches such as community-based exercises and nutritional guidance. Our site features various resources, including heart-healthy cultural recipes, updates on the latest cardiovascular research, and a beginner's guide to improving heart health for South Asians, an exercise guidebook and a cohesive explanation on why YOUR HEART HEALTH is so important. By fostering a community-centric platform, DilDost aspires to create a supportive space where users can ameliorate and accelerate their cardiovascular health journeys.
