About Us

Dil Dost came to be due to an English assignment. It's always English Assignments that spur Ananya Shrivastava to create and follow a new direction. With a history in mental health advocacy, Ananya noticed the disparities in not just mental, but cardiovascular health for South Asian Americans too. After extensive research and writing a paper on the health care system regarding the cardiovascular health of South Asians, our founder decided to further her initiative and continue advocacy through DilDost. Her mission is to help SAAs to be able to understand and care for their heart health amidst this hidden cardiovascular public crisis.
